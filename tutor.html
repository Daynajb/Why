TUTOR ORIEL

09/2022 Solo Project 8 weeks

For this project we were given a very poorly designed website for an online tutoring service. The site was poorly organized, the sign up flow was frustrating and the design was rudimentary and gave an untrustworthy feel to the brand. My job was to analyse the site, make note of industry norms, research my users, create prototypes, test those prototypes in interview and synthesise until I had a proposal for how the site could be designed to work better.

THE OUTCOME

I designed a website with a system that was much clearer and more functional than the initial product. Users could now browse the site before being requested to sign up or pay for classes that they didn’t understand. I also devised a student portal so that students could organsise their classes, schedule, homework, notes and correspondance.

LESSONS

With the time constraints of this project I had to chose only three main areas to focus on fixing.

Unexpected insights arose during my testing around preferences and expectations. This led me to design too many complex solutions. I found I had to reign myself in and refine my designs.

FUTURE STEPS

I would like to create a mobile version of this site.

I would like to address the other problem areas that could not be addressed due to time constraints
